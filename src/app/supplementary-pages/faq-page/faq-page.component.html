 What is the difference between Inverter vs. Non-Inverter?

The difference is inverter aircon costs more than non-inverters but are inherently more energy-efficient and will help you save on your monthly bills.

While the inverter aircon is the better investment and the more reliable choice in terms of energy efficiecy, the non-inverter aircon is much more accessible, especially for those who won’t be using the air conditioner too often.

 Is it okay to have the AC running 24/7? Is it more efficient? You may use it as long as you desired. However, the savings will depend, for example, if there is no one using it but the unit is ON, it is still better to turn it off because you will save more on having no consumption at all than having just low consumption. This applies to conditions wherein there is a big interval between the last usage and the next usage of your AC. Which is better? Window type or split type aircon? Both unit types have their own advantage and disadvantages. A window type is easier to install, cheaper, and requires less maintenance but they tend to be noisier, and especially slower to cool than a split-type aircon & not energy efficient due higher electricity bill. In most cases, the energy efficiency ratio of the split AC is higher than the window AC. Therefore, split AC is more energy-efficient than window AC.
